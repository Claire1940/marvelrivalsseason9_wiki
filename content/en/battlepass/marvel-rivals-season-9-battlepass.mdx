<Section icon={Swords} title="Season 9 Balance and Regenerative Shields">

Season 9 introduces a fundamental shift in survivability through the **Regenerative Shield** system. This new health type is designed to reward tactical disengagement and cover usage. Unlike standard health, which requires a Strategist to restore, Regenerative Shields begin to recover automatically after a player avoids damage for 5 seconds. This change significantly impacts the **marvel rivals season 9 battlepass** meta, as players must now choose heroes who can effectively cycle their shield pools.

**Global Ultimate Energy Adjustments:**
- **Vanguards/Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from 70% to 55%.
- **Strategists**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from 75% to 60%.
- **Strategists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from 75% to 55%.
