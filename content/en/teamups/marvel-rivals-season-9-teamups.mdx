<Section icon={Shield} title="Regenerative Shields and Vanguard Meta Shifts">

One of the most transformative updates in **marvel rivals season 9 teamups** is the introduction of **Regenerative Shields**. This new health type is designed to reward players who use cover effectively and know when to disengage from a losing fight. Unlike standard health, these shields begin to recover after a short period of safety.

**How Regenerative Shields Work:**
1. **Avoid Damage**: You must stop taking damage for 5 seconds.
2. **Auto-Recovery**: After the delay, the shield recovers all chargeable points over the next 5 seconds.
3. **Healing Interaction**: Standard healing still applies to the base health pool, but shields manage themselves.

| Vanguard Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Captain America** | 300 | 300 | 600 |
| **Hulk** | 400 | 300 | 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | 650 | 0 (Buffed Shield) | 650 (High Barrier) |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 850 | 0 | 850 (Pure Bulk) |
| **Venom** | 400 | 250 | 650 |

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Tip">
When playing Captain America or Hulk, use your mobility (Leap or Shield Dash) to retreat behind a corner once your shield is broken. Five seconds of safety is equivalent to a full health pack in Season 9.
</Callout>
