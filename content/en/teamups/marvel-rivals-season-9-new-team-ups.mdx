<Section icon={Shield} title="Regenerative Shields and Vanguard Meta">

Season 9 introduces **Regenerative Shields**, a new health mechanic that rewards smart positioning and "hit-and-run" tactics. This change primarily affects Vanguards, shifting them away from being pure "damage sponges" toward a more dynamic playstyle where they must weave in and out of combat to maximize their survivability.

| Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Captain America | 300 | 300 | 600 |
| Hulk | 400 | 300 | 700 |
| Doctor Strange | 650 | 0 (Buffed Base) | 650 |
| Devil Dinosaur | 850 | 0 (Buffed Base) | 850 |
| Venom | 400 | 400 | 800 |

The mechanic is simple: after avoiding damage for 5 seconds, your Regenerative Shield begins to recover. It takes another 5 seconds to fully charge from zero. This means heroes like Captain America and Hulk can engage, take significant shield damage, retreat behind a corner or a Doctor Strange barrier, and return to the fight with full health without taxing their Strategists' resources.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Advice">
If you are playing a hero with Regenerative Shields, communicate with your Strategist. Let them know when you are backing off to let your shields recharge so they can focus their healing on Duelists who don't have innate recovery mechanics.
</Callout>
