<Section icon={Shield} title="How Regenerative Shields Interact with Team Ups">

Season 9 introduces a brand-new health pool mechanic: **Regenerative Shields**. This mechanic changes how frontliners sustain themselves during skirmishes and directly influences which Team-Ups you should equip.

These shields operate using the exact same logic as base Health for percentage-based healing and damage calculations. However, they feature a unique recovery mechanic:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Avoid Damage">
    Disengage from active combat and avoid taking any incoming damage for **5 seconds**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automatic Regeneration">
    Your Regenerative Shield will automatically trigger its rapid recovery phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Full Recovery">
    All chargeable shield points will fully recover over the next **5 seconds**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
