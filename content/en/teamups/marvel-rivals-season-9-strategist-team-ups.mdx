<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Season 9 Team-Up System Rebuild Explained">

Marvel Rivals Season 9: The Mystery of Thebes introduces the most massive evolution to the game's core combat systems since launch. The headline change is a complete, ground-up overhaul of the Team-Up mechanic. In previous seasons, Team-Up abilities required both specific heroes to be present on the same team and rotated out seasonally. 

Starting in Season 9, every single hero possesses two permanent, independent Team-Up loadouts. This systemic overhaul eliminates seasonal rotations and allows players to choose their preferred synergy style directly from the spawn room.

| Mechanic Feature | Old Team-Up System | New Season 9 System |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Partner Requirement** | **Mandatory** to activate any effect | **Optional** (unlocks Enhanced version) |
| **Loadout Options** | Single locked synergy per character | **Two independent loadouts** per hero |
| **In-Match Swapping** | Locked once the match begins | **Freely swappable** in the spawn room |
| **Availability** | Rotated out on a seasonal schedule | **Permanently active** and balanced individually |
| **Anchor Bonuses** | Flat health/damage buffs applied | Flat anchor bonuses **fully removed** |

<Callout type="tip" title="Sustain Strategy">
Because flat Team-Up Anchor health bonuses have been removed globally, Strategists must rely more on active ability synergies and positioning rather than passive stat padding.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Base Effects vs Enhanced Effects">

To support solo-queue players and encourage strategic flexibility, NetEase has split every Team-Up ability into two tiers of effectiveness: **Base Effects** and **Enhanced Effects**. This ensures that you are never locked out of your character's utility kit simply because your teammates chose different heroes.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
 <Card title="Base Effect">
 - **Always Active**: Functions automatically regardless of team composition.
 - **Core Mechanics**: Grants the fundamental controls, animations, and base utility of the chosen Team-Up.
 - **Solo-Queue Friendly**: Ensures solo players retain their full active toolkit.
 </Card>
 <Card title="Enhanced Effect">
 - **Partner Triggered**: Requires the designated partner hero to be actively playing in the match.
 - **Stat & Utility Buffs**: Acts as a direct numerical or mechanical upgrade to the Base Effect.
 - **Team Coordination**: Highly rewards coordinated duos and pre-made team compositions.
 </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Section icon={Target} title="Top Strategist Team-Up Loadouts to Run First">

Strategists receive some of the most gameplay-altering changes in the **marvel rivals season 9 strategist team ups** roster. From explosive ammunition to situational transformations, these loadouts dictate how support players protect their front line and pressure flankers. 

### 1. Rocket Raccoon + Groot (Explosive Healing)
This loadout converts Rocket Raccoon's standard primary fire into dual-purpose explosive rounds. 
* **Base Effect**: Rocket's primary fire deals moderate area-of-effect splash damage and heals allies caught in the blast radius.
* **Enhanced Effect (Groot Active)**: Increases the splash radius and boosts the healing-per-second values when firing near Groot.

### 2. Adam Warlock + Ultron (Quantum Expansion)
Designed for teams that require sustained support uptime during prolonged choke-point battles.
* **Base Effect**: Quantum Magic ammo capacity is permanently increased, reducing the frequency of reload windows.
* **Enhanced Effect (Ultron Active)**: Boosts Adam Warlock's maximum Quantum Magic ammo capacity from 15 to 25, allowing for unmatched healing output.

### 3. Mantis + Star-Lord (Vibrant Vitality)
A highly mobile, single-target healing boost perfect for supporting dive-heavy Duelists.
* **Base Effect**: Enhances the baseline healing-over-time value of Mantis's Healing Flower.
* **Enhanced Effect (Star-Lord Active)**: Healing Flower output scales to 15 health plus an additional 3% of the target's maximum health per second.

### 4. Loki + Hela (Villain's Illusion)
Arguably the most complex and high-ceiling utility loadout in the Strategist class.
* **Base Effect**: Loki gains the ability to briefly assume the form of a defeated enemy hero, securing access to their basic abilities.
* **Enhanced Effect (Hela Active)**: Loki can temporarily utilize the full ability kit, including the copied hero's Ultimate, upon securing a finishing blow.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide: Selecting and Swapping Loadouts">

Maximizing your impact as a Strategist requires active adaptation. Follow these steps to manage your Team-Up loadouts throughout a match:

<Steps>
 <Step num="1" title="Review Team Composition">
 Before locking your hero, look at your team's Vanguards and Duelists. Identify if any of your available Team-Up partners (like Groot, Star-Lord, or Ultron) are selected to secure the Enhanced Effects.
 </Step>
 <Step num="2" title="Select Your Spawn Loadout">
 Open the Team-Up menu in the character selection screen. Equip the loadout that best fits the map geometry and your team's defensive or offensive strategy.
 </Step>
 <Step num="3" title="Monitor Enemy Counter-Picks">
 If the enemy team runs heavy dive heroes (like Venom or Black Panther), prepare to adjust. Watch how their positioning pressures your backline.
 </Step>
 <Step num="4" title="Swap Mid-Match in Spawn">
 If your current synergy isn't working, return to the spawn room. Press the swap key to exchange your active Team-Up loadout (e.g., swapping Mantis's Star-Lord healing boost for the Adam Warlock self-sustain pact to survive aggressive dives).
 </Step>
</Steps>
