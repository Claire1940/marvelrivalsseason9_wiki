<Section icon={Shield} title="The Regenerative Shield Meta Shift">

Our **marvel rivals season 9 heroes prediction** highlights the massive impact of the newly introduced **Regenerative Shields**. This mechanic converts a portion of a hero's base health pool into a shield that regenerates automatically after avoiding damage for 5 seconds.

### How Regenerative Shields Work
1. **Trigger**: Avoid taking any damage for 5 consecutive seconds.
2. **Recovery**: The entire chargeable shield pool fully recovers over the next 5 seconds.
3. **Synergy**: Behaves exactly like base health for percentage-based healing calculations.

| Hero | Old Base Health | New Base Health | Regenerative Shield |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 | 300 | **300** |
| **Hulk** | 650 | 400 | **300** |
| **Angela** | 450 | 350 | **200** |
| **Groot** | 700 | 850 | **0** (Raw Buff) |

This system heavily favors high-mobility flankers and off-tanks who can weave in and out of cover, completely resetting their health pools without draining their team's healing resources.
