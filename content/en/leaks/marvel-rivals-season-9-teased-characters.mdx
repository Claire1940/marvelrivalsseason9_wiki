<Section icon={Shield} title="Regenerative Shields and Combat Changes">

One of the most drastic changes in **Marvel Rivals Season 9** is the introduction of **Regenerative Shields**. This new health mechanic is designed to reward players who use cover effectively and manage their engagement timing. Instead of relying solely on Strategist healing, certain heroes can now recover a portion of their durability automatically.

**How Regenerative Shields Work:**
- **Activation**: Shields begin to recover after a hero avoids taking damage for 5 consecutive seconds.
- **Recovery Rate**: Once activated, the chargeable shield points fully recover over the next 5 seconds.
- **Health Split**: Many Vanguard heroes have had their base health reduced but replaced with an equal or greater amount of Regenerative Shielding.

| Hero | Old Health | New Health + Shield | Net Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Health | **300 HP / 300 Shield** | Better sustainability in poke fights |
| **Hulk** | 650 Health | **400 HP / 300 Shield** | Increased total pool to 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | 600 Health | **650 Health (No Shield)** | Raw HP buff to compensate for lack of regen |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 Health | **850 Health (No Shield)** | Massive frontline durability boost |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Energy Nerf">
Global Ultimate energy generation has been reduced across the board. Vanguards and Duelists now convert damage to energy at 55% (down from 70%), while Strategists see a similar drop in healing-to-energy conversion. Expect fewer Ultimates per match.
</Callout>
