<Section icon={Shield} title="Systemic Reworks: Regenerative Shields & Team-Ups">

Season 9 introduces a ground-up systemic overhaul that affects over 80% of the roster. The old flat "Team-Up Anchor" stats have been completely swept away in favor of highly specialized partner interactions.

### The Mechanics of Regenerative Shields
A brand-new health type is joining the battlefield. Designed to reward tactical disengagement and high mobility, Regenerative Shields offer automatic recovery.

| Shield Property | Mechanics & Rules |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Logic Basis** | Follows identical rules to base Health for percentage-based healing and damage. |
| **Trigger Delay** | Begins regenerating automatically after avoiding all damage for **5 seconds**. |
| **Recovery Speed** | Fully recovers all chargeable shield points over the subsequent **5 seconds**. |
| **Key Recipients** | Captain America, Hulk, Thor, Venom, Angela, Rogue. |

### The Evolved Team-Up System
Every single hero in the game now possesses **two independent Team-Up Ability loadouts**, containing both a "Base Effect" and an "Enhanced Effect". 

```
[Hero Selected] ──> [Choose Loadout A or B] ──> [Active Partner Enhances to Enhanced Effect]
```

For instance, Devil Dinosaur's loadout with The Punisher grants a Base Effect allowing him to shoot enemies; when paired with Punisher, it upgrades to let Punisher ride him.
