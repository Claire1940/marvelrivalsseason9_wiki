<Section icon={Shield} title="Season 9 Balance Changes & Regenerative Shields">

Season 9 introduces a complete overhaul of global health and ultimate mechanics. The most significant system update is the introduction of **Regenerative Shields**, which reward players who use cover effectively and manage engagement timings.

| Mechanic | Old System Value | Season 9 New Value | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vanguard Ult Gain** | 70% damage-to-energy | 55% damage-to-energy | Fewer high-impact tank ultimates per match |
| **Duelist Ult Gain** | 70% damage-to-energy | 55% damage-to-energy | Requires precise kill confirmation for charge |
| **Strategist Ult Gain** | 75% healing-to-energy | 60% healing-to-energy | Slower defensive ultimate windows |
| **Regen Shields** | None | Recovers after 5s of no damage | Encourages tactical disengagement and cover |

Several prominent Vanguards have had their health pools split to accommodate this new mechanic, changing how they sustain themselves during long team fights.

| Vanguard Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective Health |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 300 | 300 | **600** |
| **Hulk** | 400 | 300 | **700** |
| **Doctor Strange** | 650 | 0 (Gains Seraphim Buff) | **650** |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 850 | 0 (Raw HP Buff) | **850** |

<Callout type="info" title="Shield Recovery">
Regenerative Shields take exactly 5 seconds of avoiding damage to trigger. Once active, they fully recover all chargeable shield points over the subsequent 5 seconds.
</Callout>
