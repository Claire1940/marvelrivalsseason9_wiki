<Section icon={Shield} title="Regenerative Shields and Patch Notes Analysis">

The most impactful change in **marvel rivals season 9** is the introduction of **Regenerative Shields**. This mechanic shifts the focus from pure "heal-botting" to smart disengagement and cover usage. Multiple Vanguards have had their base health adjusted to accommodate this new shield type.

**How Regenerative Shields Work:**
1. **Trigger**: Avoid taking damage for **5 seconds**.
2. **Recovery**: Shields begin to regenerate automatically.
3. **Completion**: Chargeable shield points fully recover over the next **5 seconds**.

| Hero | Old Health | New Health/Shield Mix | Change Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Health | 300 HP / 300 Regen Shield | Better poke resistance |
| **Hulk** | 650 Health | 400 HP / 300 Regen Shield | Improved frontline uptime |
| **Doctor Strange** | 600 Health | 650 HP (Base Increase) | Compensation for energy nerfs |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 Health | 850 HP (Base Increase) | Enhanced survivability |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Energy Changes">
Ultimate generation has been reduced globally. Vanguards and Duelists now convert damage to energy at **55%** (down from 70%), while Strategists convert healing at **60%** (down from 75%). Matches will feel slower and more tactical.
</Callout>
