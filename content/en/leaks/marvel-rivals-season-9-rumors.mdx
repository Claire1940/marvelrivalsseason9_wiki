<Section icon={Shield} title="Patch Notes: Balance and Regenerative Shields">

Season 9 introduces a fundamental shift in how health and sustain work. The introduction of **Regenerative Shields** is designed to reward players who can successfully disengage from combat. This new health type recovers automatically after a hero avoids damage for 5 seconds, fully recharging over the following 5 seconds.

**Ultimate Energy Adjustments:**
One of the most significant balance changes in the **marvel rivals season 9** update is the global reduction in Ultimate energy generation. This is intended to prevent "Ultimate dumping," where teams simply cycle powerful abilities to win objectives without engaging in core gunplay.

| Source | Old Energy Rate | New Energy Rate (S9) | Change |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Vanguard Damage** | 70% | 55% | -15% |
| **Duelist Damage** | 70% | 55% | -15% |
| **Strategist Healing** | 75% | 60% | -15% |
| **Strategist Damage** | 75% | 55% | -20% |

**Vanguard Health and Shield Reworks:**
Several Vanguards have had their base health pools adjusted to accommodate the new Regenerative Shield mechanic.

| Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 300 | 300 | 600 |
| **Hulk** | 400 | 300 | 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | 650 | 0 | 650 (Buffed Shield) |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 850 | 0 | 850 (Pure HP Buff) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Meta Shift">
