<Section icon={Target} title="The New Team-Up Ability System">

The old, rigid Team-Up Anchor system has been completely abolished in Season 9. Instead of flat health or damage buffs, players now have access to customizable, active Team-Up loadouts that can be swapped freely inside the spawn room.

### Key Rules of the New System:
- **Two Active Abilities**: Every hero now possesses two distinct Team-Up loadout options.
- **Universal Availability**: You can equip and use any Team-Up ability even if your partner character is not on your team.
- **Empowered States**: If your partner character is present in the match, your equipped Team-Up ability automatically upgrades to an "Empowered" version with massive secondary effects.
- **No More Anchors**: Flat stat bonuses (such as bonus health or healing percentages) have been entirely removed to ensure fair competitive balance.
