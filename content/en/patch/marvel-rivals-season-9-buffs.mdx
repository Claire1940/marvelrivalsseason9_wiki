The launch of Season 9: The Mystery of Thebes introduces a massive balance pass affecting roughly 80% of the hero roster. Driven by the removal of flat Team-Up Anchor bonuses and the introduction of a brand-new Regenerative Shield mechanic, NetEase has recalibrated base health pools, weapon damage, and cooldowns to keep combat competitive and dynamic. 

**Video Highlights:**
- **Regenerative Shields**: High-mobility heroes can now disengage to automatically recover lost shield pools.
- **Ultimate Energy Nerf**: Combat pacing slows down due to reduced damage-to-energy and healing-to-energy conversions.
- **Team-Up Revamp**: Flat health and damage bonuses are replaced with direct hero partner loadouts.

### Global System Changes

To balance the newly introduced Team-Up loadouts and combat mechanics, global system adjustments have been implemented for all characters.

| System Mechanic | Season 8.5 Value | Season 9 Value | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vanguard & Duelist Ult Energy** | 70% damage-to-energy | **55% damage-to-energy** | Slower Vanguard and Duelist Ultimate pacing |
| **Strategist Ult Energy (Healing)** | 75% healing-to-energy | **60% healing-to-energy** | Delayed defensive Ultimate availability |
| **Strategist Ult Energy (Damage)** | 75% damage-to-energy | **55% damage-to-energy** | Reduced offensive Ultimate generation |
| **Regenerative Shield Trigger** | Not Available | **5s out of combat** | Automatically recovers all shield points over 5s |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Pacing Warning">
Because Ultimate abilities charge significantly slower from both damage and healing in Season 9, teams must rely more heavily on positioning, standard ability cooldowns, and direct weapon accuracy to win team fights.
</Callout>
