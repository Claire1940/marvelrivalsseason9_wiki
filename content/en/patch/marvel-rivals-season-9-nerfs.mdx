### Global Ultimate Energy Generation Nerfs
Ultimate abilities will now build significantly slower from both dealing damage and healing allies. This shift forces teams to play around standard cooldowns, natural cover, and positioning rather than relying on back-to-back ultimate wipes.

| Role / Action | Old Conversion Rate | New Season 9 Rate | Total Generation Reduction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vanguards & Duelists (Damage-to-Energy)** | 70% | 55% | **-15%** |
| **Strategists (Healing-to-Energy)** | 75% | 60% | **-15%** |
| **Strategists (Damage-to-Energy)** | 75% | 55% | **-20%** |

### The Death of Team-Up Anchor Bonuses
The passive Team-Up Anchor system—which granted flat bonus health, damage boosts, or healing boosts just for having a specific teammate in the lobby—has been entirely eliminated. In Season 9, characters must rely on active, highly specific partner synergies or their adjusted base kits. 

<Callout type="warning" title="Meta Warning">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Vanguard Nerfs and Health Adjustments">

Vanguards are taking the brunt of the survival adjustments in Season 9. While some tanks receive minor damage compensation, their raw, un-burstability is being heavily toned down. Multiple frontline heroes are trading massive chunks of their base health for the new **Regenerative Shields** mechanic.

### Understanding Regenerative Shields
Regenerative Shields operate under the same rules as base health for percentage-based healing and damage. However, if a hero avoids taking damage for 5 seconds, these shields automatically recover to full over the next 5 seconds.
* **300-point shield**: Regenerates at 60 points per second.
* **150-point shield**: Regenerates at 30 points per second.

| Vanguard Hero | Base HP Change | Regenerative Shield Added | Anchor Bonus Removed | Net Health Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Venom** | 675 to 500 | +250 Shield | -150 Anchor HP | **-75 Total HP** (Harder to sustain) |
| **Hulk** | 650 to 400 | +300 Shield | -100 Anchor HP | **-50 Total HP** (Smash cost up to 3700) |
| **Rogue** | 650 to 350 | +225 Shield | None | **-75 Total HP** (Defensive Stance nerfed) |
| **Captain America**| 600 to 300 | +300 Shield | -100 Anchor HP | **-100 Total HP** (Shield uptime is key) |
| **Angela** | 450 to 350 | +200 Shield | -100 Anchor HP | **+0 Total HP** (Lower raw floor) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Sustain Tip">

### Major Duelist Balance Changes

| Duelist Hero | Specific Nerfs & Adjustments | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Rogue** | Defensive Stance damage reduction cut (60% to 55%), Southern Brawl max bonus damage reduced (90 to 75). | Reduced brawling power and less oppressive dive potential. |
| **Moon Knight** | Ultimate ability cost spiked to 3100 energy. | Far fewer ultimate activations per match. |
| **Black Panther** | Base HP reduced to 150, gains 125 Regenerative Shield. | Lower raw health pool makes him highly susceptible to instant burst. |
| **Magik** | Base HP reduced to 150, gains 125 Regenerative Shield. | Must play around cover to trigger passive shield regeneration. |
