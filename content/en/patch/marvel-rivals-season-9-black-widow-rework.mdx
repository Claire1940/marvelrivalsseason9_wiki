### The New Ultimate: Assassin's Focus
When Black Widow activates her new Ultimate, **Assassin's Focus**, she temporarily regains her high-powered sniper scope. This Ultimate operates under strict parameters to ensure high-skill expression:

| Ultimate Attribute | Mechanic & Behavior | Playstyle Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Activation Rule** | Grants ADS sniper mode for a set duration or limited ammo | Must fire quickly and accurately before the timer or bullet count expires. |
| **Bullet Piercing** | Sniper rounds pierce through multiple targets in a line | Devastating in choke points or when enemies line up behind barriers. |
| **Hitbox Width** | Significantly wider projectile hitbox | Makes landing shots much more consistent, even against highly mobile targets. |
| **Damage Falloff** | Zero damage reduction over long distances | Allows you to safely counter-snipe from extreme ranges during the Ultimate. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ultimate Limitation">
Even during Assassin's Focus, Black Widow **cannot one-shot** full-health targets with critical headshots. Do not expect instant kills; instead, use the piercing rounds to soften up grouped enemies or execute wounded targets.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Flanking and Movement Guide">

With the removal of stamina restrictions on her **Fleet Foot** passive, Black Widow's map mobility has skyrocketed. She can now sprint and super-jump indefinitely, making her one of the most elusive flankers in Season 9.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Flank">
    Use infinite sprint to navigate high-ground pathways and side alleys unnoticed. Avoid main choke points where Vanguards hold space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The High-Jump Engage">
    Execute a super-jump to get above the enemy backline. While airborne, target vulnerable Strategists or low-health Duelists.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Slam and Combo">
    Activate **Widow's Bite Slam** to crash down, dealing AoE damage. Immediately follow up with your rapid primary fire and an **Electro-Plasma Blast** to slow and finish the target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Escape and Reset">
    Use your grapple hook hook-shots or super-jump to escape back to high ground. Because your sprint is resource-free, you can quickly find a health pack or return to your Strategist for healing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
