<Section icon={Target} title="Team-Up Synergy and Nerf Analysis">

Team-Ups have always been a cornerstone of Marvel Rivals, and this emergency patch addresses several "busted" interactions that were dominating the Season 9 meta. Specifically, the Iron Man/Thor and Ultron/Peni Parker combinations were targeted to bring them back into a healthy range.

**Key Team-Up Changes:**
- **Iron Man & Thor**: The outer ring damage of the Unibeam was significantly reduced. This prevents Iron Man from melting entire teams by simply aiming in their general direction.
- **Ultron & Peni Parker**: The healing provided by drones attached to allies was slashed from 35/s to 15/s. Additionally, the cooldown was increased to 20s.
- **Mantis & Star-Lord**: Enhanced healing was reduced to (15 + 3.5% Max Health), reigning in the "invincible" dive comps.
- **Psylocke & Emma Frost**: The bonus health and illusion duration were both nerfed to prevent Psylocke from having too much effective HP.

| Team-Up Pair | Affected Ability | Change Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Iron Man / Thor | Unibeam (Outer) | 84/s down to 70/s |
| Ultron / Peni | Support Drones | 35/s down to 15/s |
| Mantis / Star-Lord | Healing Flower | 17.5 down to 15 base |
| Psylocke / Emma | Wing Shurikens | Bonus HP 15 to 12 |

<Callout type="info" title="Team-Up Note">
The Ultron nerf is particularly heavy. At 15/s healing, the drones are now more for utility and minor sustain rather than a primary defensive tool during heavy fire.
