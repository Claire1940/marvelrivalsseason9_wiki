### Global Ultimate Energy Generation Changes
To align with the massive gameplay adjustments introduced in Season 9, the efficiency of ultimate ability energy generation has been reduced across the board. This ensures that ultimate abilities are earned through active, high-impact plays rather than passive stalemates.

| Role Group | Old Energy Conversion Rate | New Energy Conversion Rate | Impact Rating |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vanguards & Duelists (Damage to Ult)** | 70% | **55%** | High Nerf |
| **Strategists (Healing to Ult)** | 75% | **60%** | Moderate Nerf |
| **Strategists (Damage to Ult)** | 75% | **55%** | High Nerf |

### The New Regenerative Shields Mechanic
Season 9 officially introduces a brand-new type of health: **Regenerative Shields**. This mechanic operates identically to base health for percentage-based healing and damage calculations, but possesses a powerful passive recovery trait.

<Callout type="success" title="Shield Recovery Rules">
If you manage to avoid taking damage for **5 seconds**, your Regenerative Shields will automatically begin generating, fully recovering all chargeable shield points over the next **5 seconds**. For example, a 300 shield pool recovers at 60 points per second, while a 150 shield pool recovers at 30 points per second.
</Callout>
