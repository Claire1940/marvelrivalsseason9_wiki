<Section icon={Target} title="Season 9 Meta Shifts and Tier List">

The meta for **marvel rivals season 9** is defined by two major changes: the introduction of Regenerative Shields and the nerf to Ultimate energy generation. Vanguards and Duelists now see their damage-to-energy conversion reduced from 70% to 55%, meaning team fights will rely more on fundamental skill and cooldown management rather than "Ultimate spam."

### Role-Based Meta Analysis
- **Vanguards**: Now focus on "sustain-and-retreat" tactics. Heroes like Captain America and Hulk have had their base health partially converted into Regenerative Shields, rewarding players who use cover effectively.
- **Duelists**: Poke damage is king. Heroes like Hela have received buffs to their base damage, making them more effective at chipping away at Regenerative Shields before they can recover.
- **Strategists**: Healing is more deliberate. With the reduction in healing-to-ultimate conversion, Strategists must choose their moments to heal more carefully to ensure they have their Ultimates ready for critical objectives.

| Tier | Heroes | Reason for Placement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S-Tier** | Doctor Strange, Luna Snow | Massive shield buffs and essential healing boost |
| **A-Tier** | Devil Dinosaur, Hela | Increased health pools and higher projectile damage |
| **B-Tier** | Iron Man, Punisher | Nerfed baseline damage but strong in specific Team-Ups |

<Callout type="info" title="Regenerative Shield Mechanic">
Regenerative Shields begin recovering after **5 seconds** of avoiding damage. It takes an additional **5 seconds** to fully recharge the shield portion of your health bar. Use this window to cycle cooldowns.
</Callout>
