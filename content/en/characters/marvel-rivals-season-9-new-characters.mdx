<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Jubilee: The Pyrotechnic Strategist">

Jubilee joins the roster on day one of Season 9 as a dynamic, high-mobility **Strategist**. Known for her signature style and explosive mutant fireworks, she bridges the gap between high-output healing and offensive crowd control. She is the second major X-Men member to arrive in recent updates, following the release of Cyclops.

### Core Ability Kit
Jubilee's gameplay is fast-paced, relying on positioning, aerial hover mechanics, and careful placement of her healing utilities.

| Ability Name | Description | Combat Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Plasma Projectiles** | Fires explosive firework-style energy bursts at enemies. | Deals moderate damage and applies a blinding effect. |
| **Healing Bubbles** | Deploys localized bubbles of energy that restore health. | Provides burst healing to grouped allies in choke points. |
| **Aerial Hover** | Allows Jubilee to hover briefly in the air after jumping. | Grants vertical safety and better angles for throwing heals. |
| **Dazzling Finale (Ult)** | Unleashes a massive, lethal firework show across the field. | Deals continuous AoE damage and displaces hostile teams. |
