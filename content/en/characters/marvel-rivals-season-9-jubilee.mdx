<Section icon={Shield} title="New Mechanic: Regenerative Shields and Health Changes">

Season 9 introduces **Regenerative Shields**, a fundamental change to how Vanguards sustain themselves on the frontline. Unlike regular health, these shields begin to recover automatically after a hero avoids damage for **5 seconds**. Once the regeneration starts, the shield fully recovers over the next 5 seconds.

This change is designed to reduce the "heal-bot" requirement for Strategists. Vanguards can now take a trade, retreat behind cover for a few seconds, and return to the fight with a fresh shield pool without taxing their support's resources.

| Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | **300** | **300** | **600** |
| **Hulk** | **400** | **300** | **700** |
| **Doctor Strange** | **650** | **0 (850 on Shield)** | **1500 (Max Barrier)** |
| **Venom** | **450** | **250** | **700** |
| **Thor** | **400** | **200** | **600** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
If you take even 1 point of damage, the 5-second timer for **Regenerative Shields** resets. Use natural cover and destructible walls effectively to ensure your shield has time to kick in.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Team-Up Pairings to Master">

The rework of the Team-Up system is the most significant strategic change in Season 9. Instead of generic buffs, specific pairs now unlock unique gameplay advantages. Understanding these pairings is essential for climbing the ranked ladder.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Core Vanguard">
    Start your draft with a strong frontline like **Doctor Strange** or **Devil Dinosaur**. These heroes serve as the "Synergy Anchors" for the rest of the team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Synergy Partner">
    Look for the specific partner that enhances your Vanguard. For example, picking **Invisible Woman** specifically to boost Strange's shield capacity by 150 points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance with Strategist Support">
    Add a Strategist that complements the pair. **Mantis** works exceptionally well with **Star-Lord**, increasing her healing output to 15 per second plus a percentage of the target's max health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Dive or Poke">
    If using **Psylocke**, pair her with **Emma Frost** to increase her bonus health on hit, allowing for more aggressive flanking maneuvers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
