<Section icon={Shield} title="Regenerative Shields and Patch Notes">

The July 10 patch (Version 20260710) introduces **Regenerative Shields**, a new health type that fundamentally alters Vanguard survivability. Unlike standard health, these shields automatically recover after a hero avoids damage for 5 seconds. This encourages a "cycle" playstyle where tanks engage, absorb pressure, and then retreat to cover to refresh their effective HP.

**Ultimate Energy Generation Changes:**
The developers have significantly slowed down the "Ultimate economy" to prevent matches from being decided solely by back-to-back Ultimates.

| Role | Source | Old Conversion | New Conversion |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Vanguard** | Damage Dealt | 70% | **55%** |
| **Duelist** | Damage Dealt | 70% | **55%** |
| **Strategist** | Healing Done | 75% | **60%** |
| **Strategist** | Damage Dealt | 75% | **55%** |

<Callout type="info" title="Vanguard Health Reworks">
Several heroes have had their base health partially converted into Regenerative Shields. **Captain America** now has 300 Health and 300 Shield, while **Hulk** boasts 400 Health and 300 Shield. This makes them much harder to "chip down" over long periods if they use cover effectively.
</Callout>

### Major Hero Adjustments
- **Doctor Strange**: Base health increased to 650; Shield of the Seraphim value increased to 850.
- **Hela**: Nightsword Thorn base damage boosted to 80; Astral Flock now allows free directional flight.
- **Luna Snow**: Removed damage boost from Number One Idol, but increased Healing Boost to 20%.
- **Rocket Raccoon**: Repair Mode healing increased to 55, but C.Y.A. Ultimate cost is higher with added distance falloff.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Season 9 Team-Up Abilities Guide">

Marvel Rivals Season 9 has removed many of the generic "Team-Up Anchor" bonuses that provided flat health or damage buffs. Instead, the focus has shifted toward **specific hero pairings** that unlock unique tactical advantages. To succeed in the Season 9 meta, players must coordinate picks to maximize these synergies.

<Steps>
 <Step num="1" title="Identify the Core Pairings">
 Look for heroes that directly buff each other's primary abilities. For example, **Doctor Strange** gains an extra 150 shield value when paired with **Invisible Woman**.
 </Step>
 <Step num="2" title="Optimize for Role Needs">
 If your team needs more sustain, pair **Adam Warlock** with **Ultron** to increase his Quantum Magic ammo capacity from 15 to 25, reducing the frequency of reload gaps.
 </Step>
 <Step num="3" title="Execute Damage Windows">
 Coordinate with your Duelists. **The Punisher** sees his baseline damage reduced this season, but pairing him with **Daredevil** restores his full damage potential through their unique Team-Up.
 </Step>
</Steps>
