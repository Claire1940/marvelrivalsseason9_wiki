<Section icon={Shield} title="Season 9 Systemic Overhauls: Team-Ups & Regenerative Shields">

**Marvel Rivals Season 9** is not just about new characters; it introduces the most significant systemic reworks in the game's history. The developers have completely redesigned how Team-Up abilities and health pools function, completely shifting the competitive meta.

### The Team-Up System Rework
Previously, Team-Up abilities required both partner heroes to be active on the same team. In Season 9, characters can now activate their Team-Up abilities independently to gain a **Base Effect**. If the designated partner is also selected, the ability upgrades to its **Enhanced Effect**.

| Hero | Selected Partner | Base Effect | Enhanced Effect |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Doctor Strange** | Invisible Woman | Standard Shield of the Seraphim | Shield gains +150 value (1000 total) |
| **Adam Warlock** | Ultron | Standard Quantum Magic ammo capacity | Ammo capacity increased from 15 to 25 |
| **Mantis** | Star-Lord | Healing Flower heals 10 + 2.5% max HP/s | Healing increased to 15 + 3% max HP/s |
| **Psylocke** | Emma Frost | Wing Shurikens grant +10 Bonus Health | Bonus Health per hit increased to +15 |

### Introducing Regenerative Shields
To balance the removal of flat Team-Up Anchor health bonuses, Season 9 introduces **Regenerative Shields** to multiple frontline Vanguards. These shields act identically to base health but regenerate rapidly if the hero avoids taking damage for **5 seconds**.

| Vanguard Hero | Old Health Pool | New Season 9 Health Pool |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Base HP | **300 Base HP + 300 Regenerative Shield** |
| **Hulk** | 650 Base HP | **400 Base HP + 300 Regenerative Shield** |
| **Angela** | 450 Base HP | **350 Base HP + 200 Regenerative Shield** |

<Callout type="success" title="Sustain Strategy">
When playing Vanguards with Regenerative Shields, utilize natural cover and team barriers to disengage for 5 seconds. Letting your shield fully recover over the next 5 seconds saves your Strategist's healing cooldowns for critical moments.
</Callout>
