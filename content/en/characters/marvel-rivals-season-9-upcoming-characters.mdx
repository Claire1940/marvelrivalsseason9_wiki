<Section icon={Shield} title="Season 9 Meta: Regenerative Shields & Balance">

One of the most significant changes accompanying the **marvel rivals season 9 upcoming characters** is the introduction of the Regenerative Shield system. This mechanic fundamentally changes how Vanguards like Captain America and Hulk engage in combat. Instead of relying purely on a massive health pool that requires constant attention from Strategists, these heroes now possess a portion of their health as a "chargeable" shield.

| Hero | Base Health | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 300 | 300 | 600 |
| **Hulk** | 400 | 300 | 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | 650 | 0 (Buffed Base) | 650 |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 850 | 0 (Buffed Base) | 850 |

The shield begins to recover after a hero avoids damage for **5 seconds**. Once the regeneration kicks in, the entire shield pool is restored over the following 5 seconds. This encourages a "hit and run" playstyle for tanks, allowing them to dive in, soak damage, retreat to cover, and return to the fight fully shielded without draining their support's resources.

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Analysis">
Doctor Strange is a massive winner in Season 9. Not only did his base health increase to 650, but his **Shield of the Seraphim** was buffed to 850 value, making him the premier "anchor" tank for defensive compositions.
</Callout>

Additionally, the global reduction in Ultimate energy generation means that "Ultimate-cycling" is no longer a viable strategy for every team fight. Players must now rely on their core ability kits and mechanical skill rather than waiting for a "press Q to win" moment.
