<Section icon={Swords} title="Jubilee Abilities and Playstyle Mechanics">

Jubilee's kit revolves around her mutant ability to generate explosive energy plasmoids. In Marvel Rivals, these fireworks are converted into dual-purpose projectiles that provide reliable team sustain while keeping enemy duelists at bay.

### Core Ability Breakdown

- **Vibrant Energy Plasmoids**: Jubilee fires firework-style projectiles that heal friendly targets upon impact while simultaneously scorching enemies.
- **Blinding Sparks**: An active ability that unleashes a flash of blinding light, disorienting enemies in a targeted area and leaving them vulnerable to counter-attacks.
- **Aerial Hover**: After performing a jump, Jubilee can briefly hover in the air, allowing her to escape ground-based hazard zones and secure better lines of sight.
- **Healing Bubbles**: Deploys localized utility zones that continuously regenerate health for injured allies seeking cover.
- **Grand Finale (Ultimate)**: Jubilee unleashes a massive, lethal firework show across the battlefield. This ultimate heals allies, damages enemies, applies a blind effect, and knocks back targets, making it a perfect tool for objective control.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Meta Shifts & Rebuilt Team-Up System">

Season 9 introduces a complete overhaul to the Team-Up system. Flat anchor bonuses have been completely removed and replaced with specific, selectable partner loadouts. This change ensures that team synergy is more active and dynamic than ever before.

### Key Team-Up Synergy Pairings

- **Doctor Strange + Invisible Woman**: Shield of the Seraphim gains an additional 150 shield value, boosting the barrier to a massive 1000 total health.
- **Elsa Bloodstone + Devil Dinosaur**: Increases Elsa's Double-Barrel Blaster pellet damage from 4.5 to 5, boosting close-range burst potential.
- **Adam Warlock + Ultron**: Boosts Quantum Magic ammo capacity from 15 to 25, minimizing reload gaps during heavy team fights.
- **Mantis + Star-Lord**: Healing Flower potency improves significantly, raising the healing output to 15 plus 3% of the target's maximum health per second.

### Global Balance & Health Type Adjustments

| Balance Change | Old Value | New Value | Meta Impact |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Vanguard/Duelist Ultimate Gain** | 70% energy from damage | **55% energy from damage** | Slower Ultimate cycles; rewards tactical cooldown usage |
| **Strategist Ultimate Gain** | 75% energy from healing | **60% energy from healing** | Support players must fight actively rather than heal-botting |
| **Captain America HP Type** | 600 Base Health | **300 Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** | Rewards hit-and-run tactics; shield recovers after 5s of safety |
| **Hulk HP Type** | 650 Base Health | **400 Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** | Frontline tanks can sustain themselves without constant heals |
