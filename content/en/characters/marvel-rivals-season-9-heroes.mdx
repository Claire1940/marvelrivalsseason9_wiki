<Section icon={Shield} title="New Mechanic: Regenerative Shields">

Season 9 completely alters frontline combat by replacing raw health pools with **Regenerative Shields** for several prominent Vanguard heroes. This mechanic encourages a "hit-and-run" defensive playstyle.

| Hero | Old Health Pool | New Health Pool (Season 9) | Key Balance Adjustments |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Base Health | **300 Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** | Encourages tactical retreats to let shields recharge. |
| **Hulk** | 650 Base Health | **400 Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** | Heavy Blow damage increased to **45**; Ultimate cost increased. |
| **Doctor Strange** | 600 Base Health | **650 Health + Base Buffs** | Shield of the Seraphim value increased from 700 to **850**. |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 Base Health | **850 Base Health** | Massive raw health buff to offset lack of passive shield. |

### How Regenerative Shields Work
1. **Identical Scaling**: They behave exactly like base health regarding percentage-based heals and damage modifiers.
2. **The 5-Second Rule**: If a hero avoids taking damage for **5 consecutive seconds**, the shield begins recovering automatically.
3. **Rapid Recharge**: Once the regeneration starts, all missing chargeable shield points fully recover over the next **5 seconds**.

<Callout type="info" title="Sustain Meta">
Vanguards with Regenerative Shields must cooperate closely with their Strategists. Retreating behind natural cover for just 5 seconds can completely restore half of your effective health pool without draining your support's healing resources.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Systemic Team-Up Overhaul & Meta Shifts">

The development team has completely revolutionized the Team-Up system. Instead of passive, flat anchor bonuses (such as flat health or raw damage boosts), every hero now has **two independent Team-Up loadouts** that players pre-select before a match. These loadouts introduce completely new standalone abilities or replace existing base skills.

### Top Season 9 Team-Up Pairings
- **Doctor Strange + Invisible Woman**: Grants Doctor Strange's Shield of the Seraphim an extra **150 shield value** (reaching 1000 total).
- **Elsa Bloodstone + Devil Dinosaur**: Increases Elsa's Double-Barrel Blaster pellet damage from 4.5 to **5.0**.
- **Adam Warlock + Ultron**: Boosts Quantum Magic ammo capacity from 15 to **25**, reducing reload windows.
- **Mantis + Star-Lord**: Enhances Healing Flower potency to **15 + 3% of target's max health per second**.
- **Psylocke + Emma Frost**: Grants Psylocke **15 Bonus Health** per hit (up from 10) when using Wing Shurikens.
