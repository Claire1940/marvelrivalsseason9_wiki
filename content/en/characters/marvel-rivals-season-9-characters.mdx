### Jubilee's Complete Ability Kit
Jubilee joins the roster as a highly mobile, aggressive Strategist. She manipulates colorful light plasmoids to support her team while pressuring flankers.

- **Energy Plasmoids (Primary Fire)**: Fires light blasts that simultaneously heal allies and damage enemies. Triggering a Sparkle Mark boosts her attack speed and allows her to fire without consuming ammo.
- **Sparking Sprint**: Wraps Jubilee in protective vampiric energy, granting a massive movement speed boost and letting her fire Energy Blasts at zero cost. Upon ending, it triggers a massive fireworks field that damages and knocks back nearby enemies.
- **Hover Movement**: Holding the jump button allows Jubilee to briefly hover in the air, granting her unique vertical vantage points to heal her team.
- **Vampiric Field**: Deploys a localized zone that grants Life Steal to all allies standing inside when they attack enemies.

Black Widow has historically struggled to find a consistent place in high-level competitive play due to the feast-or-famine nature of her sniper kit. In Season 9, she receives her first-ever ground-up character rework, transforming her from a passive sniper into a highly active, high-mobility Duelist.

### Black Widow Kit Adjustments

| Ability | Previous State | Season 9 Reworked State |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard State** | Had manual ADS sniper mode with one-shot potential. | Sniper mode removed from basic kit; primary fire is now a fast-firing automatic rifle. |
| **Charged Shot** | Only available during her Ultimate ability. | Now a standard cooldown ability, firing a piercing electro-plasma blast that slows enemies. |
| **Ultimate Ability** | Enhanced her standard weapon output. | Becomes her **ADS Sniper Mode** for a limited duration or until a set number of high-damage, piercing shots are fired. |

<Section icon={Shield} title="New Mechanic: Regenerative Shields">

To balance the pacing of combat and reduce reliance on raw healing output, Season 9 introduces **Regenerative Shields**. Multiple **marvel rivals season 9 characters** have had their base health pools partially converted into this new shield type.

### Regenerative Shield Mechanics
- **Functionality**: Operates exactly like base health for percentage-based damage and healing effects.
- **Trigger Condition**: If a hero avoids taking damage for **5 seconds**, their Regenerative Shield begins to rapidly recover.
- **Recovery Rate**: All chargeable shield points are fully recovered over the ensuing **5 seconds**.

### Affected Frontliners & Tanks

| Hero | Previous Health Pool | Season 9 Health & Shield Mix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Base Health | **300 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** |
| **Hulk** | 650 Base Health | **400 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield** |
| **Angela** | Standard Health | **Reduced Base Health (-100) + 200 Regenerative Shield** |
| **Venom** | Standard Health | **Reduced Base Health (-175) + 250 Regenerative Shield** |

<Callout type="success" title="Tactical Advantage">
Regenerative Shields reward smart positioning and cover usage. Frontline tanks can now dive into a fight, absorb damage, retreat behind a barrier or wall for 5 seconds, and rejoin the fray with up to 300 health self-regenerated without exhausting their Strategist's cooldowns!
</Callout>
