<Section icon={Swords} title="Team-Up Ability Overhaul Explained">

Perhaps the most significant gameplay change in **marvel rivals season 9** is the complete redesign of the **Team-Up Ability system**. Previously, activating a Team-Up required both specific heroes to be present on the same team. 

In Season 9, players can now freely select and activate a Team-Up ability from **two independent loadouts** even if their designated partner is not on the team. Doing so grants a functional **Base Effect**. However, if your designated partner is also selected by a teammate, the ability upgrades into its **Enhanced Effect**, offering massive stat boosts and bonus mechanics.

Additionally, the old flat "Team-Up Anchor" bonuses (such as passive +100 Health or +10% Healing Boosts) have been entirely removed to ensure individual hero balancing is much cleaner.

| Character Synergy Pair | Base Team-Up Effect | Enhanced Partner Effect (Partner Present) |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Doctor Strange + Invisible Woman** | Deploys a standard Shield of the Seraphim | Shield gains **+150 shield value** (1,000 total shield) |
| **Elsa Bloodstone + Devil Dinosaur** | Standard shotgun blast spread | Double-Barrel Blaster pellet damage rises from **4.5 to 5** |
| **Mantis + Star-Lord** | Standard Healing Flower output | Healing improves to **15 + 3% of target max HP per second** |
| **Adam Warlock + Ultron** | Standard Quantum Magic healing cycle | Quantum Magic ammo capacity increases from **15 to 25** |
| **Psylocke + Emma Frost** | Gain small shield from Wing Shurikens | Bonus Health from shurikens increases from **10 to 15 per hit** |

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy Check">
Always coordinate with your team during the hero selection phase. While you can use Team-Up abilities solo now, running the optimal partner pairs still provides a distinct competitive advantage in high-tier ranked lobbies.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Version 20260710 Balance Updates & Patch Notes">

The Season 9 balance patch introduces a brand-new mechanical layer: **Regenerative Shields**. This health type functions identically to normal health for healing and damage calculations, but automatically begins regenerating if a hero avoids taking damage for **5 seconds**. The entire shield pool will fully recover over the following 5 seconds, rewarding smart positioning and cover usage.

Furthermore, Ultimate generation speeds have been slowed down globally to reduce visual clutter and make tactical coordination more meaningful.

### Universal Ultimate Energy Changes
- **Vanguards & Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **70% to 55%**.
- **Strategists**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 60%**.
- **Strategists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 55%**.

### Key Hero Adjustments

| Hero | Role | Major Season 9 Balance Changes |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | Vanguard | Base health reduced from 600 to 300; gained **300 Regenerative Shield**. |
| **Hulk** | Vanguard | Base health reduced from 650 to 400; gained **300 Regenerative Shield**. Heavy Blow damage increased to **45**. |
| **Doctor Strange** | Vanguard | Base health increased to **650**; Shield of the Seraphim increased to **850 value**. |
| **Black Widow** | Duelist | **Reworked**: Removed ADS sniper mode from base kit; increased primary fire rate. Ultimate is now a timed, high-damage ADS sniper state. |
| **Hela** | Duelist | Nightsword Thorn base damage increased from **70 to 80**; Astral Flock gains free directional flight. |
| **Cloak & Dagger** | Strategist | Received major direct, field, and ultimate healing buffs across their entire kit. |
| **Luna Snow** | Strategist | Removed damage boost from *Number One Idol*; increased Healing Boost from **10% to 20%**. |
