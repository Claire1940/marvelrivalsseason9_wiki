### Jubilee (Launch Hero - Strategist)

<Section icon={Shield} title="Patch Notes: Regenerative Shields & Ultimate Tuning">

The patch balance adjustments focus on slowing down combat pacing and introducing a new health type.

### Global Ultimate Energy Generation Changes
Ultimate abilities were charging too quickly in previous seasons. The developers have reduced energy conversion rates across the board to emphasize tactical positioning over ultimate spam.

| Role Type | Action | Old Conversion Rate | New Conversion Rate |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Vanguard** | Damage-to-Energy | 70% | 55% |
| **Duelist** | Damage-to-Energy | 70% | 55% |
| **Strategist** | Healing-to-Energy | 75% | 60% |
| **Strategist** | Damage-to-Energy | 75% | 55% |

### Regenerative Shields Mechanic
This new health pool automatically recovers after avoiding damage for 5 seconds, regenerating all chargeable shield points over the next 5 seconds.

| Hero | Base Health Change | Shield Added | Total Effective Health |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | Reduced from 600 to 300 | +300 Regenerative Shield | 600 |
| **Hulk** | Reduced from 650 to 400 | +300 Regenerative Shield | 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | Increased from 575 to 650 | None | 650 |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | Increased from 750 to 850 | None | 850 |

<Callout type="warning" title="Sustain Warning">
Heroes relying on Regenerative Shields must utilize natural cover. If you take even 1 point of chip damage during the 5-second recovery window, the regeneration timer resets completely.
</Callout>

</Section>
