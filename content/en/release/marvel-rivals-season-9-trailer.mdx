The centerpiece of the **marvel rivals season 9 trailer** is **Thebes**, a brand-new **Convoy** map set to release on **July 23, 2026**. This Egyptian-themed battlefield is deeply tied to the lore of Apocalypse and features a mix of ancient architecture and advanced celestial technology.

### Map Design & Strategic Layout
Thebes is structured around narrow chokepoints and towering vertical platforms, offering unique advantages to highly mobile heroes.

  <Step num="1" title="The Excavation Site">

  <Step num="2" title="The Temple Gates">
