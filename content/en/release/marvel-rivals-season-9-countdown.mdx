<Section icon={Shield} title="Meta Shifts & Balance Patch Notes">

Version 20260710 introduces massive system overhauls that will redefine high-level competitive play. The core adjustments target ultimate economy, individual hero survivability, and the complete restructuring of team compositions.

### 1. Global Ultimate Energy Changes
Ultimate abilities will be significantly slower to charge across all roles. The developers have reduced the conversion rates of damage and healing into Ultimate charge to emphasize core neutral gameplay and cooldown management:
- **Vanguards & Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **70% to 55%**.
- **Strategists**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 60%**.
- **Strategists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 55%**.

### 2. The Introduction of Regenerative Shields
To combat poke damage and reward tactical retreats, several frontline heroes trade a portion of their base health for a new **Regenerative Shield** pool. These shields automatically begin recovering after a hero avoids taking damage for **5 seconds**, fully recharging over the subsequent **5 seconds**.

| Hero | Old Health Pool | New Season 9 Health Pool |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Base Health | 300 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield |
| **Hulk** | 650 Base Health | 400 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield |
| **Doctor Strange** | 600 Base Health | 650 Base Health (Plus 850 Shield of Seraphim) |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 Base Health | 850 Base Health (No shield) |

### 3. Revamped Team-Up Synergy
NetEase has removed many flat "Anchor" bonuses (such as generic health or damage boosts) to focus on highly specific, tactical hero pairings. Selecting complementary characters now unlocks unique mechanical benefits:

| Team-Up Pairing | Active Synergy Effect | Tactical Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Doctor Strange + Invisible Woman** | Shield of Seraphim gains **+150 shield value** (1000 total) | Ultimate choke-point defense |
| **Elsa Bloodstone + Devil Dinosaur** | Double-Barrel Blaster pellet damage increases from **4.5 to 5** | Close-range tank shredding |
| **Adam Warlock + Ultron** | Quantum Magic ammo capacity increases from **15 to 25** | Sustained healing output |
| **Mantis + Star-Lord** | Healing Flower improves to **15 + 3% of target max HP/sec** | High-mobility dive support |
