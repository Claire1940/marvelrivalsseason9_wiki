<Section icon={Shield} title="New Mechanic: Regenerative Shields">

To shift the competitive meta away from pure burst damage, Season 9 introduces a brand-new defensive health type: **Regenerative Shields**. This mechanic rewards smart positioning and defensive disengagements.

### How Regenerative Shields Work

<Steps>
 <Step num="1" title="Base Health Logic">
 Regenerative Shields share the exact same calculations as base Health. Any percentage-based healing or damage applies to shields identically.
 </Step>
 <Step num="2" title="The 5-Second Rule">
 If a hero avoids taking any damage for **5 consecutive seconds**, the automatic recovery process begins.
 </Step>
 <Step num="3" title="Rapid Recovery Window">
 Once activated, the shield will rapidly regenerate, fully recovering 100% of its maximum capacity over the next **5 seconds**.
 </Step>
</Steps>

### Hero Health Allocation Changes

Multiple Vanguards have had their raw health pools adjusted to accommodate this new mechanic:

| Hero | Old Health Pool | New Season 9 Health Pool |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 Base Health | 300 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield |
| **Hulk** | 650 Base Health | 400 Base Health + 300 Regenerative Shield |
| **Doctor Strange** | 575 Base Health | 650 Base Health (No shield, flat buff) |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 Base Health | 850 Base Health (Flat survivability buff) |

<Callout type="info" title="Sustain Meta Shift">
Vanguards with Regenerative Shields should rotate behind physical barriers or natural map cover when their shields break. This allows them to fully regenerate 300 health in just 5 seconds without taxing their Strategists' healing cooldowns.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Systemic Team-Up Overhaul & Balance Changes">

The biggest mechanical shift of Season 9 is a complete, ground-up rework of the **Team-Up System**. Instead of flat, passive stats, Team-Up bonuses are now highly dynamic.

### The Evolution of the Team-Up System
- **Dual Loadouts**: Every hero in the game now possesses two completely independent Team-Up ability loadouts.
- **Base vs. Enhanced**: Each loadout features a baseline active effect and an enhanced version triggered by specific partner synergies.
- **Anchor Bonus Cleanup**: Flat stat buffs (like Doctor Strange's old +150 Team-Up Anchor Health) have been entirely removed to prioritize active, skill-based synergies.

### Highlighted Team-Up Synergies

| Hero Pair | Loadout Name | Team-Up Synergy Effect |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Magneto + Hulk** | Gamma Blade | Hulk infuses gamma energy into Magneto's metallic rod, letting Magneto launch a gamma sword that triggers a chain of massive explosions. |
| **The Punisher + Devil Dinosaur** | Dino Rider | Grants a base shooting ability to Devil Dinosaur; when Punisher is near, he can actively mount and ride Devil Dinosaur into battle. |
| **Hulk + Wolverine** | Fastball Special | Wolverine can be thrown by Hulk directly into the enemy backline, triggering his fury state independently of his normal cooldowns. |

### Ultimate Energy Generation Adjustments
To balance the fight pacing alongside these new synergies, NetEase has slowed down how quickly players earn their Ultimate abilities:

- **Vanguards & Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion efficiency reduced from **70% to 55%**.
- **Strategists (Healing)**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 60%**.
- **Strategists (Damage)**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 55%**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tactical Warning">
Because Ultimates build significantly slower in Season 9, teams must rely on basic ability cooldowns and coordinated Team-Up combos to win neutral skirmishes rather than waiting to cycle Ultimate abilities.
</Callout>
