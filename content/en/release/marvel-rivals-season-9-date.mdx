<Section icon={Shield} title="Patch Notes: Regenerative Shields and Balance Changes">

The **marvel rivals season 9 date** brings one of the most significant mechanical changes in the game's history: **Regenerative Shields**. This new health type is designed to reward tactical disengagement. Multiple Vanguards have had their base health partially converted into these shields, which begin to recover automatically after a player avoids damage for 5 seconds.

| Hero | Base Health Change | Regenerative Shield | Total Effective HP |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Captain America** | 600 → 300 | 300 | 600 |
| **Hulk** | 650 → 400 | 300 | 700 |
| **Doctor Strange** | 650 (No Shield) | 0 (Buffed HP) | 650 (High Base) |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | 750 → 850 | 0 | 850 (Raw Tank) |

In addition to health changes, Ultimate energy generation has been nerfed across the board. The goal is to make "Ultimates" feel like game-changing events rather than frequent spam. 

**Ultimate Energy Generation Nerfs:**
- **Vanguards/Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from 70% to 55%.
- **Strategists**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from 75% to 60%.
- **Strategists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from 75% to 55%.

<Callout type="warning" title="Meta Warning">
Vanguards like Hulk and Captain America now rely on "hit-and-run" tactics. If your Regenerative Shield is broken, you **must** find cover for 5 seconds to initiate the recovery process or risk being easily eliminated.
</Callout>
