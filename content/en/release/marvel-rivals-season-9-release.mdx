<Section icon={Swords} title="Systemic Reworks: Team-Up Overhaul & Black Widow">

The developer team has introduced a massive, ground-up rework of the **Team-Up system**, completely changing how team compositions function in high-tier competitive play.

### Two-Loadout Team-Up System
Instead of rigid single-character pairings, every single hero in the game now possesses **two independent Team-Up loadouts**. Each loadout features a "Base Effect" and an "Enhanced Effect". To balance this structural shift, developers have removed many flat "Anchor Bonuses" (such as passive bonus health or general damage boosts) and replaced them with specific, interactive partner rewards.

| Hero Pair | Loadout Focus | Mechanical Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dr. Strange + Invisible Woman** | Barrier Sustain | Shield of the Seraphim gains +150 value (1000 total) |
| **Elsa Bloodstone + Devil Dinosaur** | Close-Range Burst | Double-Barrel Blaster pellet damage rises from 4.5 to 5 |
| **Adam Warlock + Ultron** | Healing Rhythm | Quantum Magic ammo capacity increases from 15 to 25 |
| **Mantis + Star-Lord** | High-Target Healing | Healing Flower output rises to 15 + 3% of target max HP |

### Black Widow Character Rework
Black Widow receives a massive redesign to change her playstyle from a passive backline sniper into a highly active, mobile skirmisher. 

- **Stamina Removal**: Stamina restrictions on both her sprint and super jump have been completely removed.
- **Widow's Bite Slam**: A brand-new airborne ability allows her to grapple toward a target location and slam down for high AoE damage.
- **Sniper Adjustments**: She can no longer one-shot on critical headshots. However, the hitbox width of her sniper bullets has been drastically increased, and they can now pierce through multiple targets with no damage falloff at range.

<Callout type="info" title="The Goal of the Rework">
These changes make Black Widow much more forgiving to play for average players while rewarding those who utilize her newly unlocked high-speed vertical mobility.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Season 9 Patch Notes & Meta Shifts">

The balance landscape has shifted dramatically due to two major gameplay adjustments: the introduction of **Regenerative Shields** and a global reduction in **Ultimate Ability energy generation**.

### Global Ultimate Energy Nerf
Ultimates will be popped significantly less often in Season 9. The efficiency of converting damage and healing into Ultimate energy has been reduced across all roles:
- **Vanguards & Duelists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **70% to 55%**.
- **Strategists**: Healing-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 60%**.
- **Strategists**: Damage-to-energy conversion reduced from **75% to 55%**.

### Regenerative Shields Mechanic
A brand-new health type, **Regenerative Shields**, has been added to multiple frontline heroes. They inherit the exact same logic as base Health for percentage-based interactions, but they automatically regenerate if the hero avoids taking damage for **5 seconds**. The entire shield pool fully recovers over the following **5 seconds**.

| Hero | Base Health Change | Regenerative Shield Added | Compensation / Other Changes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Captain America** | Reduced from 600 to 300 | **+300 Shield** | Team-Up Anchor 100 Bonus Health removed |
| **Hulk** | Reduced from 650 to 400 | **+300 Shield** | Heavy Blow damage increased from 40 to 45 |
| **Doctor Strange** | Increased from 575 to 650 | None | Shield of the Seraphim value increased to 850 |
| **Devil Dinosaur** | Increased from 750 to 850 | None | Critical hit damage reduction buffed |
| **Groot** | Increased from 700 to 850 | None | Frontline health pool heavily buffed |

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Takeaway">
Vanguards with Regenerative Shields must master "cover-dancing". Ducking behind destructible walls or barriers for just 5 seconds will completely restore half of your health pool without forcing your Strategists to waste valuable healing cooldowns.
</Callout>
