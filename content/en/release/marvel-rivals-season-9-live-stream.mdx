<Section icon={Shield} title="Massive Team-Up System Overhaul">

One of the biggest announcements during the **marvel rivals season 9 live stream** was the complete restructuring of the Team-Up system. Instead of flat anchor bonuses that simply boosted health or raw damage, NetEase has introduced a deeper, two-loadout system for every character.

### Base vs. Enhanced Effects
Every hero in Season 9 possesses two distinct Team-Up loadouts. These loadouts are divided into a **Base Effect** (active universally or with minor conditions) and an **Enhanced Effect** (triggered only when the specific partner hero is on your team).

```
[Hero Selected] 
 │
 ├─► Base Effect (Active by default)
 │
 └─► Enhanced Effect (Triggers when matched with specific partner)
```

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Global Balance Changes and Regenerative Shields">

The live stream detailed sweeping balance adjustments coming in Version 20260710. The meta is shifting away from rapid Ultimate spam toward tactical neutral play, supported by the introduction of **Regenerative Shields**.

### How Regenerative Shields Work
A brand-new health type is being distributed across multiple frontliners to reward smart disengagement:
1. **Trigger Condition**: Avoid taking damage for **5 consecutive seconds**.
2. **Recovery Phase**: Chargeable shield points automatically recover over the subsequent **5 seconds**.
3. **Healing Rules**: Follows the same scaling logic as base health for percentage-based healing.

### Ultimate Energy Generation Reductions
To slow down the pace of late-game fights, ultimate charge rates have been significantly reduced across all roles:

| Role Class | Action Type | Old Energy Rate | New Energy Rate |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Vanguard** | Damage-to-Energy | 70% | **55%** |
| **Duelist** | Damage-to-Energy | 70% | **55%** |
| **Strategist** | Healing-to-Energy | 75% | **60%** |
| **Strategist** | Damage-to-Energy | 75% | **55%** |

<Callout type="info" title="Slower Ultimates">
With Ultimate abilities charging up to 20% slower, teams must rely on clean positioning, cooldown management, and coordinated Team-Up triggers rather than waiting for ultimate abilities to bail them out.
</Callout>
